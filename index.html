Hello there, I'm a Graphic/UI/UX Designer & Prototype Developer from Cavite, Philippines. I have been a graphic designer for more than 7 years now 5 years in printing and 2.5 years in web design. I find making web designs more interesting, challenging and fun because of the way it makes me think on how the user-experience and systems will work both front-end and back-end. I started my full career as web graphic designer at FilSupport, where I spend more than two (2) years of my time crafting and working on awesome projects. Building a very creative web pages and prototypes for web and mobile applications. One of my goal and dream is to build a web or mobile applications or development that can help and benefit the people who uses them.

When I am not working as a graphic designer, I spend my time doing some other creative stuff such as writing songs, poems, stories and doing lots of thinking. I also like travelling and going to heritage places where I will learn their histories, riding on my motorbike and do some fishing and nature tripping. I'm also famish for knowledge so normally you will find me on my computer browsing about web development and other interesting stuff because learning is a continuous process and I'm eating them all.

My Process

Nah, I'm just kidding... this is how the way I really process...

    Gathering information about the projects both front-end and back-end depending on the project that will be making.

    Start with "PEN AND PAPER" for writing ideas for the mock up or wireframe of the whole project. Layout draft and developing prototype base on the idea.
